# Adjusting Color

*From Gordon Strong, "Brewing Better Beer" — Finishing Beer. Part of [[final-adjustments]].*

## Darkening

To add **dark color and a smooth roasty flavor without bitterness or harshness**, use **Weyermann SINAMAR** — essentially liquid **Carafa Special II** extract.

- Packaged **sterile** — open and use directly, no boiling needed.
- **Once opened**, add it to the **late boil** to sterilize. Store opened bottles in the **fridge or freezer**.
- **Experiment with small additions** to see the color contribution — a very light touch of something dark often gives a **reddish hue**.

See [[characterizing-malt]] for the huskless/debittered roasted malts this is derived from.

## Lightening

If the beer is too dark, you're largely limited to **blending** *(excluded from this wiki)* or **dilution** (rarely worth it).
