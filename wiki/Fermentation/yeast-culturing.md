# Yeast Culturing Gear

*From German brewing and more*

A simple [Cynmar](http://www.cynmar.com) order list for equipment useful for yeast culturing. Cynmar offers very competitive prices and stocks nearly everything needed. This list was compiled in 2009 — exact item titles may have changed.

---

## Basic Equipment

| Item | Notes |
|------|-------|
| Erlenmeyer Flask, 500 ml (6-pack) | Only sold as a 6-pack — split with other brewers or keep them all. Very useful to have on hand. |
| Pyrex Heavy Duty Erlenmeyer Flask, 2000 ml | Essential for larger starters and boiling Speise. |
| Glass Petri Dish, 75×17 mm (10-pack) | Pick up more than one pack. |
| Clear Glass Vials with Autoclavable Cap, 12 ml (12-pack) | Pick up more than one pack. |
| Test Tube Brush, White Nylon, 3" brush / 8" total | For cleaning vials and tubes. |
| Nichrome Inoculating Loop, 25 gauge wire, 8" total length | For streaking plates. |
| Nichrome Inoculating Needle, 25 gauge wire, 8" total length | For stabbing slants. |
| Dehydrated Agar Powder, 30 g | Can also be found cheaper at health food stores. |
| Glass Alcohol Lamp with cap, 4 oz | Or use a propane torch or gas stove as an alternative. |

---

## Advanced Equipment

| Item | Notes |
